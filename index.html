Making AI
accessible
to everyone

Breaking down barriers to artificial intelligence through free, open-source solutions

We believe artificial intelligence should be a universal right, not a privilege. As a non-profit organization, we're dedicated to providing free AI tools and services to communities worldwide, breaking down barriers and democratizing access to transformative technology that can improve lives and create opportunities.

Founded on the principle that technological advancement should benefit all of humanity, COREA STARSTROUPE works tirelessly to eliminate the digital divide in AI access. We partner with educational institutions, healthcare organizations, small businesses, and community groups to provide cutting-edge AI capabilities without the traditional barriers of cost, technical complexity, or geographic limitations.

Our mission is built on three core pillars that guide every decision we make.

These foundational principles ensure that our work remains focused on creating meaningful, sustainable impact while maintaining the highest standards of ethical AI development and deployment.

01

Universal access

Providing free AI tools and services to underserved communities, students, researchers, and organizations worldwide. We believe everyone deserves access to cutting-edge technology regardless of economic circumstances, geographic location, or technical expertise.

Through partnerships with educational institutions, NGOs, and community organizations, we distribute AI resources, provide comprehensive training programs, and support local initiatives that leverage artificial intelligence for social good and economic development. Our platform serves users in over 50 countries, with multilingual support and culturally-sensitive implementations.

02

Open innovation

Developing open-source AI solutions that can be freely used, modified, and distributed by anyone. Our commitment to transparency ensures that AI development benefits humanity as a whole, not just a select few with proprietary access to advanced technologies.

We publish all our research, share our methodologies, and collaborate with the global AI community to accelerate innovation. Our open-source approach enables developers worldwide to build upon our work and create solutions tailored to their local needs and challenges. Every line of code, every model, and every dataset we create is freely available under permissive licenses.

03

Ethical development

Ensuring AI development prioritizes human welfare, privacy, and fairness. We're committed to creating AI systems that are transparent, accountable, and designed to reduce inequality rather than amplify it. Every system we build undergoes rigorous ethical review and bias testing.

Our ethical framework guides every aspect of our work, from data collection and model training to deployment and monitoring. We actively work to identify and mitigate bias, protect user privacy, and ensure our AI systems are beneficial for all communities, especially marginalized populations. We maintain strict data governance policies and never monetize user data.

Our Research Stack

Three foundational technologies that power our mission to democratize AI access worldwide. These open-source tools enable us to develop, deploy, and maintain free AI services for communities in need, ensuring scalability, reliability, and accessibility across diverse global contexts.

Our research infrastructure is built on principles of openness, collaboration, and sustainability. Each component has been carefully selected and optimized to maximize impact while minimizing resource requirements, enabling us to serve millions of users worldwide without compromising on quality or performance.

Our Researches

Our open-source research infrastructure enables us to develop and distribute free AI tools globally. These technologies form the backbone of our mission to democratize artificial intelligence and ensure equitable access to transformative technology for all communities.

Each component has been battle-tested in real-world deployments across diverse environments, from high-speed urban networks to low-bandwidth rural connections, ensuring reliable performance regardless of infrastructure limitations or geographic constraints.

Community AI Platform

Open-source platform for community-driven AI development and collaboration, enabling local organizations to build and deploy AI solutions tailored to their specific needs and cultural contexts.

Our flagship platform serves as the foundation for democratized AI access, providing intuitive interfaces, comprehensive documentation, and robust support systems that enable non-technical users to harness advanced AI capabilities.

Open AI Models

Freely available large language models and AI systems designed specifically for educational use, research, and social impact projects in underserved communities worldwide.

Our model library includes specialized AI systems optimized for low-resource environments, with particular focus on languages and dialects often overlooked by commercial AI providers, ensuring truly inclusive global coverage.

Global AI Network

Distributed computing network that provides free AI processing power to researchers, students, and organizations working on projects that benefit society and advance human knowledge.

Our distributed infrastructure leverages volunteer computing resources and strategic partnerships to provide enterprise-grade AI capabilities to organizations that could never afford such resources independently.
